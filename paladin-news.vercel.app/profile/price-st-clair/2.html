About

Price St. Clair is the Opinions Editor for The Paladin as well as the host of Red, White, Blue, & Purple, a podcast covering politics and policy from a Furman perspective. A political science major, Price is also involved with the Riley Institute and the Tocqueville Program and was a 2021 summer intern for The Dispatch, a digital media company in Washington, DC.

You can follow Price St. Clair on Twitter at @PriceStClair1.
